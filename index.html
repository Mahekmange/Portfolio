About Me

I'm a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast currently pursuing my degree in Cyber Security Engineering (3rd Year). My journey in technology started with curiosity about how systems work, which evolved into a deep interest in understanding how to protect them.

I specialize in penetration testing, web application security, and digital forensics. When I’m not hunting for vulnerabilities or solving CTF challenges, I’m building secure web applications and sharing knowledge through technical articles.

My goal is to contribute to making the digital world safer by identifying security flaws before malicious actors can exploit them. I believe in continuous learning and staying updated with the latest security trends and attack vectors.
